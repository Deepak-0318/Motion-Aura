Cookie Policy

Understand how we use cookies to provide a seamless browsing experience

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or run more efficiently, as well as to report tracking information to site owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We classify the cookies used on our website into three main categories:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are technically necessary for our website to operate. They enable core site functionalities, such as page navigation, secure area access, and session integrity (e.g. remembering whether you have closed introductory animations). Our site cannot function correctly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect aggregated details about how visitors interact with our website. This includes tracking page views, bounce rates, visit durations, and navigation paths. We use this data to identify site improvements and measure performance. The information collected is anonymous.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitor activity across websites. They allow us to serve highly relevant advertisements on external platforms (like Facebook, Instagram, or Google Search) and measure the conversions driven by our ad campaigns.

3. Third-Party Cookies We Integrated

In addition to our first-party cookies, we use components from external partners that set their own cookies on your browser:

  • Google Analytics: Tracking user behaviors, durations, and entry page routes.
  • Meta Pixel: Measuring conversion metrics for ad campaigns and building custom marketing audiences.

4. Managing and Controlling Cookies

Most modern web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can configure your browser to block cookies, alert you when a cookie is placed, or delete existing cookies. To manage cookie preferences in your browser, refer to the respective help links:

  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that blocking essential cookies may impact your user experience and cause sections of our website (including navigation and interactive items) to stop working correctly.
